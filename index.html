About me

I received the B.Eng. degree in automation from Qingdao University, Qingdao, China, in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in navigation, guidance, and control with Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2022. From Dec. 2020 to Nov. 2021, I was a joint Ph.D. Student with the Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands. My research interests include spacecraft formation flying, multi-agent systems, distributed control, event-triggered control, and adaptive dynamic programming.
